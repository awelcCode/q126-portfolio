About Me

Designer, music lover, hiker, Portlander.
Curious and resourceful, pessimistic yet infectiously enthusiastic.

I'm a Portland-based Visual Designer specializing in the intersection of brand identity and digital product experience.

Right now, I'm designing for Rejuvenation, a Williams-Sonoma brand, where I work across site UI/UX, marketing campaigns, and digital storytelling. Recent projects include refreshing the visual identity of our influencer program and prototyping new shopping tools that make complex configurations feel intuitive.

Before Rejuvenation, I spent nearly six years at a fair trade B2B company where I worked on everything from responsive web design to art direction for photoshoots in South America. I rehabilitated the company image starting with the print catalog, refined the email strategy, and leaned into motion design to help tell more compelling stories about artisan partnerships.
