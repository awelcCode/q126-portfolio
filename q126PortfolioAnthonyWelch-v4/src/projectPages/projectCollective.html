01. Project Intro

Project Collective Rebrand

Project Collective is Rejuvenation’s influencer partnership program, spotlighting the creative voices who bring our products to life in real spaces. I performed creative work for the rebrand of the landing and partner pages, working closely with cross-functional partners to elevate the visual narrative.

This project came from a functional request: to allow for multiple featured projects per partner.I introduced tabbed navigation to solve this in a way that fit the current site style.

02. Concepting

Bringing Our Very Best

As I was presenting, I could sense the group had appetite for something more. I had done some preliminary moodboards and wireframes around what I imagined Project Collective could feel like, inspired by a handwritten style in combination with editorial elements to represent a creator's notes and inspiration.

Early project moodboards
Early project wireframes

The team responded positively to the directions, approving an expanded scope from a minor update to a full visual and experiential rebrand of the program.

03. Competitive Analysis

Gathering Research and Other Strategies

Negina, Maria, and I researched competitor experiences. We noticed that most other competitors did not brand their project partnerships to this level. We were additionally encouraged by the human-first designs presented by Lulu and Georgia and Ralph Lauren.

Competitive Research: Lulu and Georgia vs. Ralph Lauren
04. Refining Our Direction

Getting Over The Finish Line

In preparation for review, I addressed a licensing challenge by replacing the previous Heatwood font, ultimately selecting Adobe Handwriting Ernie for its balanced, handcrafted style.

Typography and editorial layout exploration

Leadership were excited about the work, and we worked through minor edits with them, quieting down certain areas of the visual style.

05. Design Outcomes

Stronger Alignment & Conversion

The final design successfully bridged the gap between collaborator expertise and Rejuvenation’s "Project Authority" pillar. By showcasing the unique voices of DIY tastemakers through handcrafted moodboard elements, handwritten quotes, and personality-driven bios, we transformed a standard landing page into a creative hub. This editorial approach allows each partner's personality to shine while maintaining a rigorous brand voice through consistent iconography and refined typography.

Beyond aesthetics, the redesign prioritizes deeper community engagement and conversion. We integrated dynamic project imagery and shop-the-look calls-to-action that mirror the core site’s sophisticated style, encouraging longer browsing sessions. By celebrating the design process itself—rather than just the finished room—the new Project Collective ecosystem inspires users to see Rejuvenation not just as a retailer, but as a partner in their own creative journey.
